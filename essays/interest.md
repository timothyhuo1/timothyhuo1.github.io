##
Entering my software engineering class, I didn’t really know what to expect. However, I knew the style and format of the class would be different. In my previous information technology system classes, we focused on the fundamentals of different languages. When I think of software engineering, I think of creating applications and designs with technology. That is one of the reasons why I’m interested in software engineering because it’s a combination of skills I learned before and applying them to a final product.

## Main Interest
During the past year, there as been many opportunities and hackathons I found to be very interesting but lack the skills to participate. I saw many ideas that people have done, and it peaked my interest in software engineering. With the little experience I have in web design, it’s fascinating to see how sites such as square space and Shopify function. They portray so many different designs and templates, showing how powerful web design is with animations and interactive behavior. Looking at popular sites, it is amusing to see how they form their design to attract customers and stand out. A similar skill to web design that I hope to work on is app development. Other than making standard websites and apps, I hope to explore developing games. I have played many ios games when I was little, and always been my interest to create a simple game myself.
