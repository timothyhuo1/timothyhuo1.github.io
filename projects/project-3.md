In my senior year of high school, our engineer tech II class partipated in 2019 SSFM Tiny House Design Challenge. We were taksed to design and build a tiny house with a theme of sustainability. As a class, we all came up with designs and chose one to continue and build. It was designed as a studio apartment, aiming towards college students and young adults. 

My task in this project, I helped build the tiny house and prepare the presentation. This was my first hands on project, I learned how to operate tools and tricks to construct objects.
